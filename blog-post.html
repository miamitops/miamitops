BLNRB - Berlin to Nairobi

Posted by 9 Inch Records on 17 May 2014. 16 comments

BLNRB brought together three of the most successful electronic bands from the German club scene in combination with some of Kenya's most successful hip hop artists.

Abbas worked on tracks for the album with German bands Jahcoozi, Modeselektor and Teichmann Brothers, as well as Kenyan artists Nazizi, Ukoo Flani, Lon Jon, Kimya and Michel Ongaro.

The resulting album opened up a brand new sound to the global club scene. Abbas had the opportunity to travel to Germany as part of the BLNRB project to perform at the Worldtronics event in Berlin, and consequently went on to perform further afield in Germany, Finland and Switzerland.
